Budget

Creating a personal budget will help you keep control of your finances and work out how much money you need to put away on a regular basis to meet your savings goals.

Budget Forecast allows you to:

  • create your own customised budget items,
  • synchronise your budget across multiple devices, and
  • maintain and update your budget as needed.

Accrual balance

The accrual balance computes the estimated forecast of your credit vs debit and your savings throughout the year, visible as a list or chart. Now you can easily optimise your savings!

Forecast

The forecast helps you anticipate the money you have to save for current and future expenses as soon as you receive an income. You will never have any surprises at the end of the month!

Income / Expense

The income / expense calculates the percentage represented by each budget category and its respective cost for a defined period, e.g. monthly. Now you can easily see how much your daily coffee is costing you money per year, or how much your annual insurance costs per day!
